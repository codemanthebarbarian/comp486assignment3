Carnival

Visit for the rides, stay for the adventure.

Controls

Story/Options

Keyboard: Up and Down Arrow keys to select items, enter key to submit.

Mouse: Click on item to submit.

Touch Screen: Tap on a item to submit.

Carnival Map

Keyboard: Arrow Keys to move. I key for Inventory. P key for settings

Mouse: Move cursor away from character in direction to move and press mouse to move.

Touch Screen: Tap in away from character in direction to move.

Bumper Cars

Keyboard: Left and Right Arrow Key to turn, Up key to move forward.

Mouse: Move cursor away from car in direction to move and press mouse to move.

Touch Screen: Not Supported.

Shooting Gallery

Keyboard: Arrow keys to move crosshairs, Spacebar to shoot.

Mouse: Crosshairs will follow mouse, click to fire.

Touch Screen: Not Supported.
